Hotdogs

Ingredients

  • Hotdog buns
  • Hotdog weiner sausages
  • Alternatively, other sausage types will work.
  • Condiments

Steps

  1. Cook sausage on a hot grill, in a frying pan, or boiling in water.
  2. Prepare bun by making a cut lengthwise the bun, and 3/4 with depth.
  3. Place the cooked sausage in the bun.
  4. Garnish with condiments as desired.
